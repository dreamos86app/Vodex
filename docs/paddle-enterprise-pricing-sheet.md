## Human-driven services

**Vodex is primarily a self-serve software platform.**

Standard subscriptions (Free, Starter, Pro, Infinity) **do not** include:

- Done-for-you software development  
- Consulting or agency services  
- Human-driven app building on behalf of the customer  

Enterprise customers may receive **onboarding, technical support, or account assistance**, but the core deliverable remains **software access and AI-powered generation tools**, not bespoke human development labor unless separately agreed in writing.
